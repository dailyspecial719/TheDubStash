

	Propagation means to reproduce an organism through natural or unnatural processes. When it comes to Cannabis plants, this is considered the 3rd happiest moment for indoor growers.

	The 1st happiest moment is harvesting your hard earned fruits or flowers and the 2nd happiest moment is knowing that your plant is a female.

	Propagating your Cannabis plants can happen in two different ways: By germinating seeds or by taking clones from existing Cannabis plants. Both have their advantages and disadvantages. For the most part, they require similar equipment to get the job done so we'll be going over both of them in great detail.

The idea behind Propigation is to increase the survival rate of your plant. By controling the overall environment of your growroom, transitioning between growth phases won't be as stressful on you or your Cannabis plants.
